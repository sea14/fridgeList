Fridge List



Description of what our web application does: Fridge List allows people with busy schedules to quickly and easily create grocery lists. Once you create an account, you can save grocery lists for later use. As a user, you are also allowed to save the URLs of recipes that you find online under your account profile. Together use your saved information to determine what ingredients you still need for your latest culinary creations!
